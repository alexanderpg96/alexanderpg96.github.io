Laravel Framework, LAMP Stack, Google API

https://yourunity.org

YourUnity, a web application that brings college students and their communities together.

Through this service, Community Service organizations within San Diego can post and advertise their events to everyone on the YourUnity mobile application.

Co-founded by myself and one other student at SDSU, the service is developed and ran by a small team of three.

The Laravel Framework was chosen due to our team's previous projects in PHP, and has allowed us to scale and develop the platform at a much faster rate than other frameworks available.

If you would like to check out YourUnity, check out our website, where you can also request access to the service if you are a community service provider within the San Diego area.
