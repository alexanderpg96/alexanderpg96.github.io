iOS App, Android App

YourUnity, a mobile application that brings college students and their communities together.

Through this service, volunteers within the San Diego area can find and register for events to register at.

Co-founded by myself and one other student at SDSU, the application is solely developed by myself using the Ionic Framework, which is based on Apache's open-source Cordova framework.

The Ionic framework was chosen due to the necessity of deploying both an Andoid AND iOS application as quickly as possible. This framework has allowed me to scale and develop the platform at a much faster rate than coding for both platforms natively.

If you would like to check out the YourUnity mobile application, you can download it for iOS or Android where you can also register for events if you are within the San Diego area.
